How does it work?

These comfortable, washable, yet high-tech garments have sensors knitted into the fabric that detect your heart rate, ECG, temperature and more.

By remotely capturing this health data through your smartphone app, you can share this information with our team and/or your loved ones from the comfort of your home.

 
 
 
What are the benefits?
 

Because the Skiin Connected Life app allows you to share your data with me, we can proactively track your health metrics and ECG data should you have an abnormal rhythm, palpitations or other symptoms related to your heart.

The app also has a diary feature that lets you track daily symptoms. As a Health Canada approved Class II medical device, you may be able to receive reimbursement through your benefits provider.

The garment kit costs $275 CDN and includes 2 garments, the pod, charger and access to the app.
